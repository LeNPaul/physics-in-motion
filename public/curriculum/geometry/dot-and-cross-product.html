Dot and Cross Product

There are two kinds of vector multiplication: scalar product (also called the dot product) and vector product (also called the cross product).

Dot Product

The dot product is defined as the following:

$$ \vec{A}\vec{B} = A B \cos \phi$$

where \(\phi\) is the angle between \(A\) and \(B\), and it is between \(0^\circ\) and \(180^\circ\)
